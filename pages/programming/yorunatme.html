yo.runat.me
By Matt Bierbaum
Our english-word based URL shortener

Do you find the addresses from URL shorteners like bit.ly and goo.gl impossible to remember? Well, we've got a URL shortener for you. When you go to yo.runat.me, we suggest an English word (typically a noun) for a shortened URL. And better yet, this URL turns into an english sentence - "Yo, run at me, bro". We regret that we don't report click statistics, or have many of the other features of other shorteners, but yah know. Maybe sometime.

You can also specify your own noun by going to yo.runat.me/<noun>. If it is not taken, you can fill in the URL to which it forwards. Enjoy.
